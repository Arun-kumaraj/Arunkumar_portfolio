Product Sales Analysis Dashboard

I developed a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard using Power BI, leveraging a dataset that spans various products, buyer accounts, sales dates, and sales rate reports for the years 2023 and 2024. This dashboard provides insights into sales performance across multiple countries, allowing for easy tracking of sales trends over time. Key features include dynamic visualizations that illustrate sales growth, region-specific performance, and product-wise breakdowns. It also includes buyer demographics, enabling targeted marketing strategies. The dashboard is interactive, enabling stakeholders to filter data by country, year, and product categories. This project demonstrates my expertise in data visualization and my ability to draw actionable insights from large datasets.
